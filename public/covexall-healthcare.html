The Science

How Microplatelets Kill Pathogens

Three synergistic kill mechanisms deliver persistent antimicrobial protection long after alcohol has evaporated.

Physical Destruction, Not Chemical Evaporation

Unlike alcohol that evaporates in seconds, our copper-infused magnesium hydroxide microplatelets (200nm × 100nm × 10nm) physically bond to skin and mechanically destroy pathogen biofilm membranes on contact. The microplatelet survives the encounter and continues killing.

Three Active Kill Mechanisms

Mg(OH)₂ microplatelets create massive reactive surface area. Infused copper ions punch holes in cell membranes and destroy DNA/RNA. Benzalkonium chloride (0.12%) disrupts cell membranes at the molecular level. All three work in unison.

Persistent Residual Activity

BZK produced 3.75–4.16 log₁₀ reductions at 1, 2, and 3 hours post-application in clinical studies. The ethanol comparator showed less than 1 log₁₀ reduction at every time point. This persistent activity reduces incidental contamination between patient contacts.

Infection Control

Clinical Benefits for Your Facility

Persistent protection addresses the real-world gaps in alcohol-based hand hygiene programs.

Eliminate Incidental Contamination

Between patient contacts, practitioners touch keyboards, bed frames, chairs, and fixtures. A persistent antimicrobial hand sanitizer reduces the opportunity for microbial transfer during these incidental contacts — something alcohol cannot do after evaporation.

No Fire Code Restrictions

Alcohol-based sanitizers at FDA-recommended 60-90% concentrations are fire hazards requiring consultation with fire authorities and adherence to strict placement codes. Covexall is water-based and non-flammable — mount dispensers exactly where your workflow needs them.

Reduce HAI Risk Vectors

Healthcare-associated infections affect millions annually. Persistent hand hygiene between every patient contact, surface touch, and procedure creates a continuous barrier that evaporating alcohol simply cannot maintain.

Staff Compliance & Comfort

Staff who sanitize 50+ times per day suffer cracked, painful hands from alcohol — reducing compliance. Covexall moisturizes skin, is painless on wounds, and requires fewer applications. Better compliance = better outcomes.
